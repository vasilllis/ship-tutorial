# Before the session (please do this in advance!)

To avoid losing time to setup, please do these three things beforehand (~5 min):

1. **Check your CERN account works** — log in at <https://cern.ch> (username + 2FA).
2. **Activate CERNBox** (needed for SWAN): visit <https://cernbox.cern.ch> once.
3. **Test SWAN:** go to <https://swan.cern.ch>, log in, **Start my Session**, and
   open the **terminal** (top-right). If you get a `bash` prompt, you're ready.

Bring your laptop (any OS — everything runs in the browser). No programming needed.
